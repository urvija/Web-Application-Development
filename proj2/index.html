Happy Days Summer Camp!!

Summer Camps are here !!

Happy Days Summer Camp is a professional organisation committed to create an environment conscious society through innovative outdoor-based programmes.

One-of-a-kind classes and camps are offered in San Diego for children and teens. The behind-the-scenes summer adventures are wide-ranging, featuring hands-on classes, camps, seminars, explorations and games created by educators, performers and artists. Students have the opportunity to learn about varied disciplines.

Camps offered this year at Happy Days are:

  • Basketball Camp
  • Baseball Camp
  • Physical Training
  • Band Camp
  • Swimming
  • Nature Discovery

Happy Days' summer camps begin in mid-June and continue through August.
